Overview

Served as the Lead Intern overseeing a team of 5 interns divided into two groups: Web Team (3 interns) and AR Team (2 interns). I led the Web Development Team and collaborated cross-functionally with finance, operations, and compliance units to deliver a digital internal audit portal for PT Aneka Coffee Industry.

Project Scope

  • Duration: 3 months (Aug–Oct 2024)
  • Team: 5 interns (3 Web, 2 AR)
  • Deliverables: Web prototype, UI/UX design, system workflow, and AR demo.

My Role & Leadership

As the Lead Intern, I acted as the bridge between the Quality Control department (Users) and the IT Department.

1. Web Team Leadership

Led a 3-person Web Development Team. I directed task assignments, conducted daily progress check-ins, and managed weekly alignment meetings. I also coordinated with the AR team to ensure feature compatibility.

2. UI/UX Design & Requirements

Designed complete UI/UX flows for the audit portal (dashboard, form pages, review flow). I collected and clarified requirements from the field supervisor and prepared wireframes to keep alignment between expectations and development.

3. Project Scheduling

Created the internal project timeline for the Web Team, managed feature priorities, and ensured deliverables aligned with overall internship milestones.

4. Full-stack Contribution

Developed key frontend components and page layouts while assisting the backend team with API integration and database mapping. Provided code reviews and debugging support for junior team members.

Results & Deliverables

  • ✅ Completed Web Team deliverables on time.
  • ✅ Met all requirements validated by field supervisor.
  • ✅ Successfully led 5-member intern team structure (Web + AR).
  • ✅ Web prototype approved for internal use and included in internship scientific publication.
