Tokopedia Seller Onboarding

Reducing Time-to-First-Sale by 35% through Progressive Disclosure.

Role

Product Researcher

Timeline

4 Weeks

Focus

UX Strategy

The Problem

New sellers on Tokopedia were dropping off at a rate of 60% during the registration process. The forms were too long, asking for banking details before the user even listed a product.

The Solution

I proposed a "Progressive Onboarding" flow. Instead of asking for everything upfront, we let sellers list their first item immediately and only asked for verification details when they received their first order.

  • Removed 4 fields from initial sign-up.
  • Added a "Completion Meter" to gamify the profile setup.
  • Created automated email nudges for incomplete profiles.

The Result

Based on the prototype testing with 15 users, the Time-to-First-Product-Listing decreased from 12 minutes to 8 minutes (35% improvement).

CampusSwap

0-to-1 Product Concept: P2P Marketplace for University Students.

Role

Product Manager

Type

MVP Concept

Focus

Market Strategy

Market Opportunity

Students spend average Rp 2.000.000/semester on textbooks that they only use for 6 months. Existing solutions (Facebook Marketplace) are riddled with scams and lack campus verification.

Product Vision

CampusSwap creates a closed-loop marketplace where users must verify their Student ID (.ac.id email) to trade. This creates high trust and enables hyper-local meetups on campus.

Go-To-Market Strategy

  • Phase 1: Launch at 1 University (pilot) using Campus Ambassadors.
  • Phase 2: Partner with Student Executive Boards (BEM) for credibility.
  • Monetization: Freemium model (free listing, pay for "Urgent" highlight).

ACI Audit Portal

Digitizing Internal Audit Process for Large-Scale Coffee Industry.

Role

Assoc. Product Manager

Stakeholders

50+ Internal Users

Focus

Product Delivery

The Challenge

PT Aneka Coffee Industry was using paper-based forms for factory audits. Data entry took 3 days per audit, leading to delayed decision-making on quality control.

My Role

I bridged the gap between the Quality Control department (Users) and the IT Department (Engineers). I gathered requirements, created wireframes in Figma, and prioritized features for the MVP.

Impact

  • Reduced reporting time from 3 days to Instant (Real-time).
  • Saved approximately 15 hours of manual labor per week.
  • Successfully trained 20+ staff members on the new system.
