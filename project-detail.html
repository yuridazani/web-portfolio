Overview

Portofolio ini dibuat sebagai latihan mandiri untuk membuktikan kemampuan administrasi bisnis yang dibutuhkan di dunia kerja. Menggunakan nama perusahaan fiktif IN8 Corporation, Inc. - nama yang sebelumnya digunakan dalam proyek akademik Manajemen Proyek — sebagai entitas simulasi.

Seluruh dokumen dan laporan dibuat secara nyata menggunakan tools yang dipakai profesional: Microsoft Word, Microsoft Excel, dan software akuntansi Akaunting. Tidak ada yang template kosong — semua diisi dengan data fiktif yang realistis dan konsisten satu sama lain, membentuk satu siklus transaksi bisnis yang utuh dari penawaran hingga pembayaran.

Siklus Transaksi yang Disimulasikan

Quotation dikirim → Klien setuju → Offering Letter ditandatangani → Invoice diterbitkan → Letter of Confirmation dikeluarkan → DP 50% diterima → Surat Jalan dikirim → Laporan penjualan dicatat → Pajak dihitung

Simulasi Perpajakan — Excel

File Excel 3 sheet yang mensimulasikan perhitungan pajak dasar yang paling sering dibutuhkan di posisi admin, finance, dan operasional.

PPh Pasal 21 — Karyawan Tetap

Sheet 1
  • Simulasi 2 karyawan: Karyawan A (lajang/TK0) vs Karyawan B (kawin/K1)
  • Menghitung dari gaji bruto → penghasilan neto → PKP → tarif Pasal 17
  • Komponen: biaya jabatan (5%, maks Rp 500rb), iuran pensiun 1%
  • PTKP: TK/0 = Rp 54jt | K/1 = Rp 63jt (sesuai PMK No. 168/2023)
  • Output: PPh 21 terutang per tahun & per bulan

PPh Pasal 23 — Pemotongan Jasa

Sheet 2
  • Simulasi 2 transaksi jasa: Rp 29,5 jt & Rp 8 jt
  • Tarif 2% x nilai bruto (sesuai PMK 141/2015)
  • Output: PPh 23 dipotong & penghasilan neto yang diterima
  • Kewajiban: batas setor (tgl 10) & lapor SPT Masa (tgl 20)

PPN — Pajak Pertambahan Nilai

Sheet 3
  • Perhitungan PPN Keluaran: 11% x DPP Rp 29,5 jt = Rp 3.245.000
  • Perhitungan PPN Masukan dari pembelian (metode 11/111 x harga inkl. PPN)
  • Rekap Masa Mei 2026: PPN kurang bayar / lebih bayar
  • Tarif: 11% sesuai UU HPP No. 7 Tahun 2021

Overview

Served as the Lead Intern overseeing a team of 5 interns divided into two groups: Web Team (3 interns) and AR Team (2 interns). I led the Web Development Team and collaborated cross-functionally with finance, operations, and compliance units to deliver a digital internal audit portal for PT Aneka Coffee Industry.

Project Scope

  • Duration: 3 months (Aug–Oct 2024)
  • Team: 5 interns (3 Web, 2 AR)
  • Deliverables: Web prototype, UI/UX design, system workflow, and AR demo.

My Role & Leadership

As the Lead Intern, I acted as the bridge between the Quality Control department (Users) and the IT Department.

1. Web Team Leadership

Led a 3-person Web Development Team. I directed task assignments, conducted daily progress check-ins, and managed weekly alignment meetings. I also coordinated with the AR team to ensure feature compatibility.

2. UI/UX Design & Requirements

Designed complete UI/UX flows for the audit portal (dashboard, form pages, review flow). I collected and clarified requirements from the field supervisor and prepared wireframes to keep alignment between expectations and development.

3. Project Scheduling

Created the internal project timeline for the Web Team, managed feature priorities, and ensured deliverables aligned with overall internship milestones.

4. Full-stack Contribution

Developed key frontend components and page layouts while assisting the backend team with API integration and database mapping. Provided code reviews and debugging support for junior team members.

Results & Deliverables

  • ✅ Completed Web Team deliverables on time.
  • ✅ Met all requirements validated by field supervisor.
  • ✅ Successfully led 5-member intern team structure (Web + AR).
  • ✅ Web prototype approved for internal use and included in internship scientific publication.
